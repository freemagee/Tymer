Why does Tymer exist?

The problem

The iPhone native clock app does not have a landscape mode. There is no way to increase legibility of the timer when used from a distance.

3rd Party Options

Online timers are flash based and do not work on iDevices. Most apps on iTunes are too complicated for the purpose or do not maximise the small screen.

The solution

Build a web app

Use reponsive web design techniques and a minimal design to build Tymer.

Tymer has been built to take full advantage of whatever device you use it on. The carefully chosen typeface increases legibility allowing you to use this for cooking, exercise or other activities.

Tymer has been created by Neil Magee. Source code available on GitHub.
